CHEESE-GRILLED SANDWICH


Price:- Rs.250 Rs.199/- only

About:-

The grilled cheese (sometimes known as a toasted sandwich or cheese toastie) is a hot cheese sandwich
typically prepared by heating slices of cheese between slices of bread with a cooking fat such as butter
or mayonnaise on a frying pan, griddle, or sandwich toaster, until the bread browns and the cheese melts.
