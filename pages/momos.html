About:-

Steamed momos are a type of dumpling that originated in Tibet and are commonly found in various Asian cuisines 12.
They are made by filling dough wrappers with a mixture of minced meat or vegetables along with spices and seasonings.
The momos are steamed till cooked, resulting in a soft and moist texture.
