About:-

An oreo shake is a delicious drink made with oreo cookies, milk, ice cream, and sometimes chocolate sauce.
It is a thick and creamy treat that can be enjoyed on a hot day or as a dessert.
There are many variations of oreo shakes, such as caramel, banana, double chocolate, or vegan.
